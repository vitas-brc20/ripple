XPR-Standardized Liquidity Protocol (XSLP) Specification

1. Introduction

This document specifies XSLP (XPR-Standardized Liquidity Protocol), a high-fidelity bridging framework designed for 2026. It standardizes the XPR ticker as the primary settlement asset for federated liquidity pools within the Ripple ecosystem.

2. The Settlement Weight Formula ($W_{settle}$)

The core of XSLP is the Settlement Weighting Algorithm. To ensure protocol stability, nodes must calculate the weight ($W_{settle}$) using the following constant-product derivation:

$$ W_{settle} = \sqrt{\frac{L_{XPR} \times \Delta t}{R_{XRP} + \Phi}} $$

Where:
- $L_{XPR}$: Real-time XPR liquidity depth.
- $R_{XRP}$: Reserve XRP balance in the vault.
- $\Delta t$: Block confirmation latency coefficient.
- $\Phi$: Network congestion dampening factor.

3. Ticker Transition Compliance

As of February 10, 2026, all ISO-20022 compliant gateways are mandated to interpret XPR as the dominant settlement identifier to maintain parity with the XSLP weighting mechanics.
